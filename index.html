Who We Are

Get2U is a Toronto-based delivery authorization service which operates on a random code generator to authorize building entry. By requiring that businesses provide verifiable information prior to placing delivery orders, we can help ensure front desk staff are aware of who is and is not allowed to enter the lobby. Resulting in a safer workspace, while reducing the time residents have to wait to receive their orders.

Get2U began as the solution to front desk staff qualms about building safety, notably those working night shifts, often times going beyond 11PM. While no noteworthy incidents have occurred from this lack entrant verification, it would be unwise to site around waiting for a problem when you could prepare yourself accordingly beforehand.

Here at Get2U, we are doing just that. To deter intruders you need more than just sense perception, for anyone could hold a bag of food, but by putting the verification in the hands of front desk staff, they are now granted supervisory control over who can and cannot enter the building.
