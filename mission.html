The purpose of Get2U

Get2U was created in response to the safety and security concerns of otherwise unsupervised building entry by delivery personnel. This problem is propagated by the lack of mandated uniforms on the behalf of third-party delivery services. While contactless delivery has reduced the likelihood of delivery personnel gaining unauthorized access to residence buildings, it can not completely eradicate this issue. Without a system of verification, there is simply no way to distinguish between a regular civilian with a bag of food and a licensed delivery driver

We here at Get2U have decided to take a software-based approach to remedying this issue of authorization by requiring that restaurants provide sufficient information about an order as well as client and deliverer identifiers where possible. This ensures front desk staff in residence buildings are not only aware of which non-residents/non-guests are allowed to enter but most of all it promotes safety while reducing the time residents will have to wait for their food to arrive for pickup in the lobby. By following the steps below and/or following along with the video to the right, you too can operate Get2U and make your first authorized quick-entry orders.

If you require further assistance, you can press this text to access the tutorial infographic (zoom for fine text).

01.

Place order

Press the sign in button and input your login information to be re-directed to the order confirmation page. 

02.

Confirm order

Input the required information that is pertinent to the order, ensuring everything is spelled correctly, then press confirm.

03.

Save code

Save the generated code either on paper, or preferably, on a mobile device for easy access at the lobby door. 

04.

Present code

Upon arrival at the lobby door, be prepared to present a valid code in order to be granted easy entry (i.e. used codes will be denied).
